Video Games

I am going to cheat a little bit here. I haven't played many games released this year. Instead I'll use games I played this year for the first time but they weren't necessarily released in 2022

#5

Resident Evil 2

This remake of the PlayStation 1 classic is tight, gripping and high quality throughout

#4

Death Stranding

I wasn't initially sold on this game, but once I started I found it to be unique, captivating and lived up to the hype

#3

Hollow Knight

Challenging, charming and a surprisingly deep narrative for those looking for it

#2

Elden Ring

Genre-defining, expansive and satisfying experience front-to-back

#1

Bloodborne

Atmospheric, difficult and punishing. But once you master the gameplay loop, its a joy.
